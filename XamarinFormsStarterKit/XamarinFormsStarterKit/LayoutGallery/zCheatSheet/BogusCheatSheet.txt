Shortcut syntax usage:

lorem words syntax emit

w|W 0|9  - one  word or more word will be emitted
s|S 0|9  - one  sentence or sentences word will be emitted
l|L 0|9  - one  line or lines word will be emitted  
p|P 0|9  - one  paragraph or paragraghs word will be emitted  
t- random text
s|S 0|9  - one slug  or slugs word will be emitted
